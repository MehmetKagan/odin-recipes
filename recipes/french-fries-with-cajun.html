French Fries With Cajun

Description

French fries are one of the best and most loved dishes in the world. But have you ever tried them with cajun? This recipe will blow your mind and you will feel like you found the secret of life.

Ingredients

  • 2 potatoes
  • 300 milliliters of oil
  • 1/2 tbsp of cajun
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice potatoes and put them in a bowl which filled with water. Change 2-3 times the water after it become foggy.
  2. Put potatoes over a paper towel and make them dry as much as possible. (Its important if you like crunchy fries.)
  3. Boil the oil in a pot.
  4. Put potatoes to the pot and fry them
  5. After you fry all of potatoes put french fires in a paper bag with cajun and shake. And here is your miracle fries.
